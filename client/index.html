Connection Portal

Share your experience. You are not alone.

Please answer the following 4 questions:

In what ways do you feel alone in this world? If you are comfortable, describe what this feels like and any life experiences related to this feeling.

If you were to visualize your feeling of loneliness or isolation as a creature, which of these options might it look like?

Please offer a positive message for someone who may be feeling alone, and provide an inspiring personal anecdote if you have one.

Which symbol do you feel best encapsulates your message?

Please make sure all 4 questions have been answered.
